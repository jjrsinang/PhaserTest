Press the spacebar to keep the bee alive

Context

Bee population is slowly declining around the world. This is a problem because bees are major agents of pollination, making them essential for our food production. This game helps raise awareness of this situation by means of entertainment. The game objective is to keep the bee alive - similar to what we should be aiming to do. It promotes rehydration, easily digestable, stimulates immunity, and is beneficial for all types of skins diseases
